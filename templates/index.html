üìÖ Get TV Listings By Date:

üîä Read Listings Commands:

  • "Read all listings" - Reads all listings aloud
  • "Read listings" - Reads first 20 listings
  • "Read the listings" - Alternative command
  • "Tell me the listings" - Another way to request reading
  • "Read results" - Reads filtered results

üí° Use these commands to hear listings read aloud. The app will automatically return to wake word listening after reading.

üìñ Continue Reading Commands:

üí° Use these commands to continue reading after the initial 20 listings, or to navigate through large sets of listings.

üîÑ Quick Read Again Commands:

üí° These commands work even when the app is listening for wake words - no need to say "Hey TV" first!

üí° These commands work even when the app is listening for wake words - no need to say "Hey TV" first!
